// 01  how i work

I'm a generalist by nature. I trained as a scientist — aquaculture, then microbiology — taught myself to build, and now grow on my own land. What connects those isn't a subject; it's a way of working: I read how a system behaves — a microbiome, a hillside, a codebase — closely enough to work with how it already runs.
